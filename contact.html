Frequently Asked Questions

Quick answers to common questions

You can submit news tips via email to tips@globalnews.com or call our 24/7 news desk at +1 (555) 123-4567. Please provide as much detail as possible and include your contact information if you'd like us to follow up.

For advertising inquiries, please email ads@globalnews.com or fill out the contact form selecting "Advertising & Partnerships" as the department. Our advertising team will respond with media kits and available opportunities within 24-48 business hours.

We strive for accuracy in all our reporting. If you believe we've made an error, please email corrections@globalnews.com with the article URL and specific details about the correction needed. Our editorial standards team reviews all correction requests promptly.

All current job openings and internship opportunities are listed on our Careers page. Please apply through the online portal. For general career inquiries, select "Careers & Employment" in the contact form.

We aim to respond to all inquiries within 1-2 business days. Urgent news tips are reviewed immediately. During peak periods or breaking news events, response times may be slightly longer. You'll receive an automatic confirmation email when your message is received.
