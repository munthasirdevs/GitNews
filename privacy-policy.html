Introduction

At Global News Network ("GNN," "we," "us," or "our"), we are committed to protecting your privacy and personal information. This Privacy Policy explains how we collect, use, disclose, and safeguard your information when you visit our website, use our mobile applications, or interact with our services.

Important Notice

By using our services, you consent to the data practices described in this policy. If you do not agree with our policies and practices, please do not use our services.

Scope of Policy

This Privacy Policy applies to:

  • Our website (globalnewsnetwork.com)
  • Our mobile applications
  • Email newsletters and communications
  • Social media interactions
  • Offline data collection at events
2

Data We Collect

We collect several types of information from and about users of our services:

Personal Information
  • Name and contact details
  • Email address
  • Demographic information
  • Account credentials
Technical Data
  • IP address and device ID
  • Browser type and version
  • Operating system
  • Network information
Usage Data
  • Pages visited and time spent
  • Articles read and shared
  • Search queries
  • Video viewing patterns
Cookie Data
  • Authentication cookies
  • Preferences and settings
  • Advertising identifiers
  • Analytics cookies
We Do Not Collect

We do not knowingly collect sensitive personal information such as financial data, health information, or government ID numbers unless explicitly required for specific services.

Children's Privacy

Protecting Young Users

Our services are not directed to children under the age of 16. We do not knowingly collect personal information from children under 16. If you are a parent or guardian and believe your child has provided us with personal information, please contact us immediately.

What We Do
  • Require age verification for certain services
  • Provide parental control options
  • Implement age-appropriate content filters
  • Regularly review compliance with COPPA
What We Don't Do
  • Target advertising to children
  • Collect unnecessary data from children
  • Share children's data with third parties
  • Use children's data for profiling
